CSS Zen Garden Style Guide: Team 10

Purpose/Theme

The purpose of our webpage is to create a unique CSS Zen Garden page inspired by nature. Our main objective is to create an aesthetically pleasing page from the starter html, by only updating the stylesheets. We were inspired by nature when choosing our theme of the mountains. We will be including simple graphics of mountains in our header and of araucaria trees (a type of evergreen tree) in our footers. Our colors will be greens and earthy tones to draw from the colors found in the mountains. We will also select contrasting fonts (big and bold, vs small and simple) to illustrate the diversity of nature in the mountains.
